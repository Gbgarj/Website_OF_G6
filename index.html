PHILIPPINES

Take an interactive tour of the Philippines, an enchanted archipelago. Your ticket to a world of rich cultures, stunning scenery, and kind people is available on our webpage. The Philippines, a patchwork of 7,641 islands with individual stories waiting to be explored, lies tucked away in the center of Southeast Asia.

Palawan

The islands of Palawan stretch between Mindoro island in the northeast and Borneo in the southwest.

Intramuros

Intramuros, is the 0.67-square-kilometer historic walled area within the city of Manila, the capital of the Philippines.

Chocolate Hills

It is one of the adjacent to the old walled city of Intramuros and is of the largest urban parks in Asia.

Rizal Park

Rizal Park also known as Luneta Park or simply Luneta, is a historic urban park located in Ermita, Manila.
